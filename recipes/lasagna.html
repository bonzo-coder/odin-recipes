Lasagna

Lasagne is a famous italian dish. It becomes delicious if you use this recipe

  • Pasta
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Minced meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Oregano
  • Cheese
  1. Prepare minced meat with tomatoe sauce on the pan
  2. Prepare bechamel sauce
  3. Put pasta into miska
  4. Cover it with miced meat sauce
  5. Cover it with cheese
  6. Repeat until you have three or four levels of pasta
  7. Cover with bechamel at the top
  8. Bake for 40 minutes in 180C
